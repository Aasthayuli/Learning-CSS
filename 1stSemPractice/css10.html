CSS Overflow

It is a problem of content overflowing from the fixed div.

Overflow properties

  • overflow(for overflow of image)
  • overflow-x(horizontal overflow of content)
  • overflow-y(vertical overflow of content)

overflow values

  • hidden(hiding the content)
  • scroll(adding scrollbar to read the content by scrolling)
  • auto(on overflow, automatic scroll bar comes)
  • visible(the overflowed data will be shown as it is)
