CSS Box Shadow

SHADOW FORMAT

  • Right side shadow Format
    • box-shadow: h-offset v-offset blur spread colour of shadow;
  • left side shadow formation
    • box-shadow: -(h-offset) -(v-offset) blur spread colour of shadow;
  • for multiple shadows
    • box-shadow: h-offset v-offset blur spread colour of shadow, h-offset v-offset blur spread colour of shadow;
  • for shadow inside the box
    • box-shadow: h-offset v-offset blur spread colour of shadow inset;
